Tiramisu o'clock!

Description

This classic tiramisu recipe can be made ahead of time so all the flavours soak in, and then just topped with cocoa powder when youâ€™re ready to serve. The filling is on the sweeter side so the coffee and cocoa powder help to balance this out.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Espresso
  • Coffee liqueur
  • Egg yolks
  • Caster sugar
  • Water
  • Cold Mascarpone
  • Heavy cream
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Brew coffee ad stir in coffee liqueur with the desired intensity and store in the fridge to cool off completely
  2. Make filling by cooking sugar and water mixture on medium heat. Whisk egg yolks in a seperate bowl.
  3. Once sugar syrup is cooked to 121C slowly pour it over whisking egg yolks. Mix until bowl is cool to the touch.
  4. Add in the cold mascarpone and mix until relatively smooth with no lumps.
  5. In a seperate bowl, whisk cream to a medium stiff peak.
  6. Soak ladyfingers individually in cooled coffee mixture. Place them evenly in a dish filling any gaps.
  7. Add cream mixture and spread to cover the ladyfingers. Repeat process to the rim of the dish.
  8. Sprinkle cocoa powder in a thin layer and place in fridge to set for 6 hours or overnight.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
