Winner-Winner Butter Chicken Dinner!

Description

This finger-lickin' Butter Chicken is the dreamiest creamiest Indian curry that you can make in the comfort of your own home, even down to the iconic bold orange-red color. This recipe is beginner-friendly, yet rivals your favorite restaurant's

Ingredients

  • Diced chicken breast/thigh
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Butter chicken dry cook-in sauce of your choosing
  • Heavy cream
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Sautee the onions and garlic in the butter until they are soft and golden.
  2. Add in the tomato paste and add in the Butter chicken Dry Cook in sauce packet.
  3. Dice the chicken breast/thigh, add it to the pan to fry.
  4. Add in heavy cream and mix until the chicken is evenly covered.
  5. Simmer on mediunm heat until sauce thickens.
  6. Add milk, if needed, to thin out the sauce if it too thick.
  7. Continue to stir to your desired consistency.
  8. Serve hot, with preferred side or starch.
